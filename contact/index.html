Common Questions

What happens in the consultation?

It's a focused 30-minute conversation to understand where you are, where you want to go, and whether working together is the right fit. No obligation, no hard sell — just an honest conversation.

How quickly do you respond?

All enquiries are responded to within 24–48 business hours. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach me directly.

Do you work with clients outside Albania?

Yes — the majority of my clients are international, across Malta, Dubai, the UK, and beyond. All work is managed remotely with full transparency and communication.

What's the typical timeline to get started?

After an initial consultation, most projects begin within one to two weeks. For strategy-only engagements, timelines are typically shorter.
