Social Media Manager

Full-Time Tirana, Albania On-Site

The Role

We are looking for a full-time Social Media Manager to join our team in Tirana. This role is about content, branding, organisation, and execution — not just posting. You will manage social media operations across multiple client accounts, working with brands from different industries and helping them grow with intention and consistency.

Responsibilities

  • Manage social media operations across multiple client accounts simultaneously
  • Structure and execute content strategies tailored to each brand
  • Create, schedule, and publish content across Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more
  • Brief designers and ensure all creative output meets brand standards
  • Monitor analytics, track performance, and report on growth and engagement
  • Stay on top of platform trends, algorithm updates, and industry shifts
  • Collaborate directly with Dajana on brand voice, strategy, and client communication

What We're Looking For

  • Demonstrated experience in social media management (portfolio or case studies required)
  • Strong organisational and communication skills
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines without dropping the ball
  • Solid understanding of content creation across platforms
  • Proficiency with Canva and CapCut
  • Reliable, proactive, and genuinely committed to growth
  • Based in or able to work from Tirana on a full-time, in-office basis

Why Join Us

You will work in a modern office in one of Tirana's best locations, directly alongside Dajana on real brand accounts with real results. This is not a corporate role with layers of approval — your work will be seen, your ideas will matter, and your growth will be tied directly to the agency's growth.
